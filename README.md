# Credo - Staff Khata & Salary · Support

Credo is the trusted ledger for your household staff - attendance, salary, advances, loans and bonuses for your maid, cook and driver. Hisaab barabar.

## Support
Questions, problems or feature requests: please [open an issue](../../issues) or email gargh600@gmail.com.

## FAQ
**Where is my data stored?** On your iPhone and in your personal iCloud. Credo has no servers and no accounts. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).

**How does household sharing work?** Invite a family member with a link (Household tab). The shared ledger syncs via your iCloud - both phones see the same hisaab.

**How do I cancel Credo Family?** Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Credo, or in the App Store app. Cancelling keeps the app working for one worker.
