Privacy Policy

Effective 13 July 2026

Credo ("the app") is a household staff ledger published by Himanshu Garg.

The short version

We collect nothing. Your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud. We cannot see it, and nobody else can either.

What data the app handles

Credo stores the information you enter about your household staff (names, roles, salaries, attendance, payments) in a local database on your device. If your device is signed into iCloud, this data syncs to your private iCloud database (Apple CloudKit) so it backs up and appears on your other devices, and — if you invite a family member — on their device via Apple's iCloud sharing.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, and no servers operated by the developer. We have no technical ability to access your data: your iCloud database is private to your Apple ID and encrypted by Apple.

Voice entry

Voice entry uses Apple's speech recognition on your device (or Apple's servers per your iOS settings) to convert speech to text. The developer never receives your audio or transcripts. Microphone access is used only while you hold the mic button.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive your payment details.

Data deletion

Delete a worker in the app to erase their records, or delete the app and remove Credo's iCloud data in Settings → your name → iCloud → Manage Account Storage.

Children

Credo is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

Changes to this policy will be posted at this page.
