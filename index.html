Questions

Before you download.

Is Credo really free? +

Yes — your first staff member costs nothing, forever, with attendance and manual entries included. Credo Family (₹499/year) unlocks unlimited staff, voice entry, salary slips, and household sharing.

Do I need an account or internet connection? +

No account, ever. Credo uses the Apple ID already signed into your iPhone. It works fully offline — your household data syncs to your private iCloud only when you have a connection.

Which languages does voice entry understand? +

Hinglish and Hindi — say things the way you'd actually say them, like "Kamla ko 2000 advance diya" or "Ravi ki chhutti aaj." Credo also has a manual form for anyone who'd rather type.

What happens if I get a new phone? +

Sign in with the same Apple ID and your whole khata is there — workers, attendance history, advances, everything. It's backed up to your private iCloud automatically.

Can my spouse see the same records? +

Yes, with Credo Family. Send an invite link from the Household tab and their phone joins the same ledger — no shared password, no separate account to manage.

Is there a web version or does it only work on iPhone? +

Credo is an iPhone app by design — it needs to be in your pocket for the one-tap habit to stick. There's no separate website login; this page is just where you learn about it and download it.
